hunar
Hunar…takes forward the legacy of traditional enamelled and Kundan jewellery. It’s a collection that showcases the aesthetic charm and grace of royal jewellery. a collection inspired by the colours of Rajasthan. An intricate craft of enamelling where the piece to be worked on is fixed on a stick of lac and delicate designs like flowers, birds and fish are etched on it by skilled artisans who finish with product with near perfection. The end product is the preferred choice of discerning women.
