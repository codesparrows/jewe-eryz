kundankari
Kundankari a quintessentially Indian technique, wherein layers of pure gold (Kundan) form a solid wedge around the gemstone. Mughal jewellery was a unique combination of kundan-set gems and Meenakari. Traditional Mughal style jewellery is still made in this manner, unchanged for centuries.
